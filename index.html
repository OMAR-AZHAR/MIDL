About Us

Prognosis of cancer patients can be improved through its early detection. Visually identifying cancer in its earlier stages is at times difficult, and is subject to visual interpretation by the oncologist. That introduces chances of error, which may have debilitating effect on the health of the patient. Machines can help in accurate earlier diagnosis of the cancer, and may help in proper treatment of the patient with higher chances of survival post-treatment.

Products

The MID lab is focused on medical image processing, with special emphasis on Brain Tumor, Breast Cancer and Tuberculosis (TB) detection and diagnosis. The CAD systems development covers a wide array of diseases and image modalities. The main on-going projects of MID Lab cover the following diseases and modalities.

Brain Tumor Detection Tool

BrainViz is aimed to help strengthen brain tumor care and the health system in Pakistan. It will be able to segment out various types of tumors from MRI scans, predict overall survival rates based on age and days since diagnosis, and generate a report for physicians with all relevant data inclu­ded.

Breast Cancer Detection Tool

To aid the radiologist in the analysis of mammograms, computer-aided diagnostic (CAD) systems were developed to aid the automation of breast cancer detection, localization, and classifi­cation of malignant and benign lesions in various modalities i.e. MRI, CT scans, ultrasound, etc.

Pulmonary Disease Detection Tool

We deal with the development of Al-based CAD systems to automate TB identification and other lung diseases. Our key focus is on developing reliable, trus­tworthy, and fair Al models for dependable medical imaging diagnostics.

AI Powered Dicom Viewer

MIDL provides its own AI assisted built-in dicom viewer with advanced features. Dicom viewer provides a complete package of medical imaging and diagnostics tool. It is equiped with our refined AI Models on the left pane and generic function on the right pane like zooming, rotation, invert and many more.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some recommendations to most asked FAQs

  • Why Choose MID Lab?

    The MID lab at COMSATS University Islamabad will focus on medical image processing, with special emphasis on Brain Tumor, Breast Cancer and Tuberculosis (TB) detection and diagnosis. The CAD systems development covers a wide array of diseases and image modalities.

  • Is AI reliable in medical Imaging?

    Our Deep learning Models are optimised to predict and analyse the results with > 90% accuracy. Our researchers and teams worked very hard to achieve this score.

  • What do you mean by "Tashkhees"?

    Tashkhees means diagnostics. MID Lab has made the product with the name Tashkhees which can do Breast cancer classification and localization.

  • What is BrainViz?

    BrainViz is a diagnostic tool with visualization ability created to assist the doctors as well as radiologist in early screening of tumor and non-tumor patients. The product has the ability to pre-process the input data , detect and segment the tumor for surgery with 91% dice score.

  • What do you mean by PDS? and its purpose?

    The second product is related to tuberculosis detection and identification with the name; is the Pulmonary diagnostics system (PDS). Currently the product has 95% accuracy for publicly available datasets while 85% for local datasets. The product is complete and is ready to be train and tested on local dataset
